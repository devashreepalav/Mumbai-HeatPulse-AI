## 📊 Key Insights from Mumbai Analysis
After training the AI model on Mumbai's ward data, we found:
1. **The Heat-Greenery Gap:** Wards like **G/N (Dharavi/Sion)** exhibit the highest surface temperatures due to low NDVI (Vegetation Index).
2. **Cooling Potential:** Our Linear Regression model predicts that increasing the Greenery Index by just **0.2** in high-density wards can reduce localized surface heat by up to **2.5°C**.
3. **Economic Correlation:** Higher-income wards tend to have 30% more canopy cover, highlighting an environmental equity gap in the city.
